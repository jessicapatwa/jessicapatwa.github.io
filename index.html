About me

Hello! I'm Jessica Patwa, a passionate software engineer and innovator based in Hoboken, NJ.

My journey in tech began at the Manipal Institute of Technology in India, where I earned my Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Wanting to delve deeper, I'm now advancing my expertise with a Master's in Computer Software Engineering from the prestigious Stevens Institute of Technology.

Professionally, I've had the privilege of leading a dynamic team of developers at United Overseas Bank in Singapore. From overseeing the software development life cycle to enhancing user experiences, I've always been driven by the desire to meld technology with intuitive design.

In addition to my professional endeavors, I thrive on challenging the status quo. This is evident from my hands-on projects like the IoT plant care system and the Rescue Pet Discovery Tool. These initiatives, among others, encapsulate my belief that technology can and should be harnessed to better our world.

Beyond the screens and code, I cherish community and collaboration. I've dedicated time to significant causes, such as championing the United Nations' Sustainable Development efforts and organizing engaging tech festivals.

Now, as I approach a new chapter in 2024, I'm eager to embark on opportunities where I can infuse my technical prowess with strategic insights. Whether it's building transformative software solutions or leading groundbreaking projects, I'm here to make a difference.

Feel free to explore my portfolio, and let's connect to discuss how we can innovate together.
