GHC 2023 TIPS and TRICKS

Listing down tips for the conference from my experience with GHC 2022

MAKE USE OF THE GHC CAREER PORTAL

  1. When you sign up for the conference, you'll unlock the secret passage to the incredible GHC Career Portal
  2. Upload your Resume to the GHC database (you will have the option to edit it) and Apply for jobs through the GHC career portal even before the conference.
  3. Book 1:1 slots with recruiters (look for information in GHC Social media platforms to know when these are open) Think of it as your chance to go on a professional speed date, where you're the star and the recruiters are eager to meet their perfect match—you!
  4. Make a list of companies that are hiring online and talk to them at the GHC booth if you are going in person.

REACHOUT TO RECRUITERS

  1. Reach out to recruiters even before the conference (You can find recruiters attending GHC through 1:1 sessions or names listed under job postings)
  2. Reachout to recruiters who have used #ghc #ghc2023 #vghc #vghc2023 in their posts or profiles
  3. Don't ask them to find a role for you! Never
  4. Do your research and be specific in the role you (send them a link to the job posting, etc) want to be hired for and how your skills match
  5. Pro tip: When reaching out, send them a portfolio of your skills - I created a portfolio in 2 days

PRE - CONFERENCE TIPS

  1. Join Women in tech groups like RTC (Rewrite the Code), Facebook groups for women attending GHC, etc. to know the latest updates on who's interviewing and when the 1:1 slots are opening.
  2. Practice your elevator pitch! I can't stress this enough. Be confident, concise, and clear.
  3. Don't limit yourself in what roles you are applying to, especially in your early career; stay open to all kinds of roles.
  4. Pack half-full, leave room for swag, and throw in that foot spray—because after a day of exploring, your feet will thank you! Business casuals = confidence boost; I skipped the heels for a cozier journey!

AT THE CONFERENCE - TIPS

  1. Arrive early and own those 9-5 booth hours!
  2. Stand in the queues to the company booths with a purpose and be ready to give the elevator pitch again, again, and again!
  3. Scan those company booth QR links to upload your resume; make it snappy before the conference curtain falls, as these links have an expiration date.
  4. Take notes on your chats and conversations. Stay organized, and keep those connections alive!
  5. Join the after-5 p.m. networking events for a dose of connection and fun.
  6. Take it step by step, and remember, it's all about quality over quantity. You've got this!

POST CONFERENCE TIPS

  1. Share your notes with fellow adventurers and soak up their insights.
  2. Send a "Thank you" note to recruiters you crossed paths with at the conference or interviewed with.
  3. Stay patient while interviewers get back to you. Patience is your trusty companion on this journey; your reward awaits just around the corner.
  4. Make a post about your attendance and your learnings!
